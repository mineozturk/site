150+ Online Classes Can Help You Become A Better Parent

Courses on subjects ranging from nutrition during pregnancy to teaching your child to read to health care and first aid.

Pat Bowden Aug 17th, 2022
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Envelope
  • 2

You probably know that there are plenty of online courses to teach you data analysis or computer programming, but did you know that there are also many online classes that can teach you to be a better parent?

At Class Central, we scoured the catalog to find courses on subjects ranging from nutrition during pregnancy to child development, teaching your child to read and draw, plus health care, special needs, and first aid. As with so many other topics, it turns out that if there’s a parenting topic that interests you, it’s likely that there’s a course for it. Here are all the free and paid parenting courses Class Central was able to uncover.

While some of these courses are primarily designed for teachers or health professionals, parents may also be interested.

Have you taken one of these courses? Be sure to leave a review that can help other parents and parents-to-be decide which courses to take.
