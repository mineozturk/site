160+ Online Learning Conferences in 2023

A comprehensive list of online learning conference taking place in 2023, in-person or online.

Rui Ma & Manoel Cortes Mendez
Dec 28th, 2022
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Envelope
  • 1

The pandemic prompted a surge of interest for online education. And it pushed many conferences to move to remote participation. This had a silver lining: some events made their recordings available online. Class Central compiled 500+ Hours of Free Conference Recordings on Online Learning.

As the pandemic continues to abate in most parts of the world, many online education conferences are returning to an in-person format in 2023, though some will continue to allow participants to join remotely.

Below, we’ve compiled a comprehensive list of online learning conferences taking place in 2023. We’ll continue to update the list as new events are announced. If you know of a relevant event that isn’t listed here, please share it in the comments.
